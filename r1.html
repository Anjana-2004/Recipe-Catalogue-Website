Shahi Paneer Recipe

Ingredients:

  • Paneer - 250g, cubed
  • Onion - 2 large, finely chopped
  • Tomato - 2 medium, pureed
  • Ginger-Garlic Paste - 1 tbsp
  • Cashew Nuts - 10-12 pieces, soaked in water
  • Green Chili - 1, slit
  • Yogurt - 1/2 cup
  • Cream - 1/4 cup
  • Ghee - 2 tbsp
  • Oil - 1 tbsp
  • Cumin Seeds - 1 tsp
  • Cinnamon Stick - 1 inch
  • Cardamom Pods - 2-3
  • Cloves - 3-4
  • Turmeric Powder - 1/2 tsp
  • Red Chili Powder - 1 tsp
  • Garam Masala - 1 tsp
  • Salt - to taste
  • Fresh Cream - for garnishing
  • Coriander Leaves - for garnishing

Cooking Instructions:

  1. Heat ghee and oil in a pan, add cumin seeds, cinnamon stick, cardamom pods, and cloves. Sauté until fragrant.
  2. Add chopped onions and sauté until golden brown.
  3. Add ginger-garlic paste and green chili. Sauté for 2-3 minutes.
  4. Add tomato puree and cook until oil separates.
  5. Meanwhile, grind soaked cashew nuts into a smooth paste.
  6. Add the cashew paste to the pan and mix well.
  7. Add turmeric powder, red chili powder, garam masala, and salt. Mix and cook for 2-3 minutes.
  8. Add yogurt and cream. Stir continuously and cook until the gravy thickens.
  9. Add cubed paneer and gently mix with the gravy.
  10. Cover and simmer for 5-7 minutes.
  11. Shahi Paneer is ready to serve. Garnish with fresh cream and coriander leaves.

Cooking Time: Approximately 30 minutes    

Serves: 3-4 people
