Butter Chicken Recipe

Ingredients:

  • Chicken - 500g, boneless, cubed
  • Onion - 2 large, finely chopped
  • Tomato - 3 medium, pureed
  • Ginger-Garlic Paste - 1 tbsp
  • Cashew Nuts - 10-12 pieces, soaked in water
  • Fresh Cream - 1/2 cup
  • Butter - 2 tbsp
  • Oil - 1 tbsp
  • Bay Leaf - 1
  • Cinnamon Stick - 1 inch
  • Cardamom Pods - 2-3
  • Cloves - 3-4
  • Red Chili Powder - 1 tsp
  • Turmeric Powder - 1/2 tsp
  • Coriander Powder - 1 tsp
  • Garam Masala - 1 tsp
  • Kasuri Methi (Dried Fenugreek Leaves) - 1 tsp
  • Salt - to taste
  • Coriander Leaves - for garnishing

Cooking Instructions:

  1. Heat butter and oil in a pan, add bay leaf, cinnamon stick, cardamom pods, and cloves. Sauté until fragrant.
  2. Add chopped onions and sauté until golden brown.
  3. Add ginger-garlic paste and sauté for a few minutes until the raw smell disappears.
  4. Add pureed tomatoes, red chili powder, turmeric powder, coriander powder, and salt. Cook until the oil separates.
  5. Meanwhile, grind soaked cashew nuts with a little water to form a smooth paste.
  6. Add the cashew paste to the pan and mix well.
  7. Add chicken pieces and cook until they are done.
  8. Add fresh cream, garam masala, and kasuri methi. Mix well and cook for another few minutes.
  9. Garnish with chopped coriander leaves and serve hot with naan or rice.

Cooking Time: Approximately 30-40 minutes    

Serves: 4 people
